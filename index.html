    about

    POS+ is both an online journal website and mobile application created to promote wellness. POS+ believes that everyone deserves good mental health, and we hope to achieve that by giving an opportunity to individuals with mental illness to practice to discover ways to find positive moments in their lives. Not only that, but we believe everyone deserves a support system. That's why we've implemented a chat feature to this product. When individuals are having a bad day they are able to reach out for help.
